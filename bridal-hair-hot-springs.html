The trial

The trial is where we get it right

The trial appointment is Brenda's favorite part of the bridal process — and the most important one. You come in 4–8 weeks before your wedding. You bring photos if you have them; Brenda brings 24 years of opinions about what photographs well and what doesn't. Day-of, there's no guessing. The style goes in efficiently, and you walk out knowing exactly what you'll look like walking down the aisle — because you've already seen it.

Questions brides ask

Do you travel to wedding venues in the Hot Springs area?

Yes. Brenda travels to venues throughout Hot Springs and the surrounding region to style the bride and full wedding party on-location. Travel logistics and any associated fees are confirmed when you book.

What is a bridal hair trial and do I really need one?

A trial is a dedicated appointment — usually 4–8 weeks before your wedding — where Brenda builds and refines your style with no time pressure. You leave with a photo, a plan, and zero unknowns for the morning of. Strongly recommended for every bride.

How far in advance should I book?

Wedding Saturdays in spring and fall fill early. Booking 4–8 months out is ideal. If your date is closer, reach out anyway — Brenda will let you know availability honestly.

Can you also style the mother of the bride?

Yes, and Brenda does this regularly. Mothers of the bride and groom are welcome both as part of the on-location wedding morning and as standalone in-studio appointments. Same quality, same attention, same calm.

Ready to lock in your date?

Hot Springs wedding dates book months in advance. If you have a date, don't wait — reach out now, even just to check availability, and Brenda will get back to you personally. First-time clients receive 20% off their first visit.
